We are a small family farm on beautiful Vancouver Island providing pasture raised meat to our local community

Brian and Kendra Harvey started their farming experience in Chilliwack BC in 2016. After starting with breeding registered Nigerian Dwarf goats, free range chickens, ducks and turkeys, and Red Russian garlic they decided it was time for something bigger. With a passion for farming they decided to relocate their family of 4 (plus two dogs, two cats, herd of goats, and a handful of chickens) to Nanaimo on Vancouver Island in 2020. They found a beautiful 40 acre property backing onto the Nanaimo river, and brought some new additions to the farm; a number of speckled park cattle, commercial pigs, and handful of ducks and turkeys. They have adapted the farm over the past few years and have a heavy focus on Grass fed beef, Hay, and farrow to finish pasture raised pork.
