Pasture raised canadian pork

Pigs are intelligent clean animals. We treat all of our pigs with this in mind. They enjoy environmental stimulation and social interactions. We are a farrow to finish farm, so our pigs are born and raised As with all of our meat products they are processed and butchered at a fully licensed facility with custom cuts available.

All About Our Beef

It all begins with a dream. Our speckled park cattle are a mix of commercial cows and registered breeding stock. As with all our livestock they are treated with care and respect. They range on 20 acres of lush pasture year round. Speckle Park cattle produce high quality meat. It is available for sale by 1/2, 1/4 cow or specific cut.

Our Honey

Our honey bees are located in the Cowichan Valley, where they collect pollen from a wide range of wild flowers giving our honey a natural flavor. The types we offer currently are wildflow, Blackberry, and Fireweed Honey.

We offer honey whole sale in 3kg or 1L jars
